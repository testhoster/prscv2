About Us

At Pavan Roller Skating Club, we are dedicated to promoting roller skating for all ages. Our mission is to provide a fun, safe, and engaging environment for people to learn and excel in roller skating. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, our club welcomes you to be a part of our community!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age limit for joining the club?

We accept members of all ages, from kids to adults. However, we recommend that children be at least 5 years old to join the skating classes.

Do you provide equipment for beginners?

Yes, we provide beginner-level skates and safety gear during training sessions.

Can I get personal coaching?

Yes, we offer one-on-one coaching for those who want personalized training. Please contact us for more details.
